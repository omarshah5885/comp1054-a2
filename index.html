Ferrero Rocher offers a unique, multi-layered taste experience:

Each piece has a roasted hazelnut core surrounded by a smooth, hazelnut chocolate-rich filling that is further encased in a crisp wafer which is covered by a milk chocolate shell that contains diced pieces of hazelnut.

Gold standard of perfection:

Each Rocher chocolate is wrapped in a golden foil to represent the premium status of our brand and the customers that consume it. To ensure this premium standard of taste, over 100 quality control tests and sensory analyses are carried out.

About our Company and its History:

  • Ferrero Rocher was created out of a family owned tradition dating back to 1940’s in Alba, Piedmont, Italy by Pietro Ferrero, a pastry maker.
  • Its production was driven out of a need to make specialty confections available to every body regardless of occasion or socio-economic class.
  • Rocher is a French word which means ‘rock’ or ‘boulder’ and it was named after a grotto found in a Roman Catholic shrine in Lourdes.
  • Ferrero introduced the idea of incorporating hazelnuts into chocolates, which led to its later brands like Nutella and Kinder.
  • Ferrero is the third largest chocolate producer in the world.
  • 4.6 billion Ferrero Rocher are produced each year and sold to more than 130 countries.
